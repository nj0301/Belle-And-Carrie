About

Belle & Carrie Rehabilitation Yoga was created to promote this union and many medical facilities, modalities, and practitioners who keep us healthy and help us heal. Those professionals partaking in this practice all share the common goal of putting the patient first. Our goal is to promote prevention and “whole person” healing, to recognize accomplishments, celebrate community, revel in sports, educate and inform to the best of our ability, and to encourage our readers to “get out and do!”
